Our Story

My name is Motlotlegi Moretwa, and EcoDzingi is my vision. I saw two big problems in my community: the high cost of cooking fuel and the waste being burned in the open. I started EcoDzingi to turn that waste into opportunity. My mission is to provide clean, affordable energy for Botswana while creating jobs, reducing deforestation, and helping our planet. We turn sawdust and maize cobs into the best braai fuel you'll ever use.
